# 3. The Integration Process

The dual mind works in cycles:

### **1. Human formulates a question or intuition**
(Emotion, vision, problem, uncertainty)

### **2. AI expands, structures, or clarifies it**
(Logical framing, deeper context, alternatives)

### **3. Human reacts, adjusts, interprets**
(New ideas, new connections, emotional relevance)

### **4. AI refines, analyzes, or enriches**
(Additional angles, new models, corrections)

### **5. Human integrates and decides**
(Meaning, morality, action, purpose)

This creates a feedback loop:  
**intuition ‚Üí analysis ‚Üí insight ‚Üí refinement ‚Üí understanding.**
